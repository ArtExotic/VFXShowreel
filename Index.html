About Me

With over 3.5 years in the visual effects industry, I specialize in creating complex effects and crowd simulations for feature films,television and Movies. My approach combines technical precision with artistic vision to deliver stunning visual experiences.

As a Technical Director, I focus on developing robust systems that scale effectively while maintaining creative flexibility. I'm passionate about optimizing workflows and mentoring team members in technical best practices.

When I'm not simulating destruction or managing thousands of agents, you can find me exploring new rendering techniques or contributing to open-source projects in the VFX community.
